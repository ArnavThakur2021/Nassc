Co-Creating Value for Industries

AI CoE makes significant efforts towards helping enterprises identify, validate, and implement AI solutions that deliver measurable business impact. It acts as an ecosystem enabler, helping industries to assess their processes, strategize AI adoption and guide them for an end-to-end deployment. For MSMEs, it makes enhanced efforts to make them aware about Industry 4.0 and smart manufacturing solutions through workshops and training sessions.


AI CoE focuses on practical, high-impact AI adoption, including :

Process Inefficiencies & Bottlenecks

Quality & Inspection Challenges

Cost Overruns & Productivity Gaps

Data Underutilisation

Manual or Error-Prone Operations

Scaling Digital Initiatives Beyond Pilots

How an Enterprise Can Engage with AI CoE

Step 1

  • Identify inefficiencies and AI-ready functions
  • Conduct stakeholder interviews and process mapping

  • Support PoC and pilot implementation
  • Ensure alignment with IT and operational teams
  • Establish performance metrics and governance
Step 5

ENABLE — Build Internal AI Capability

  • Run capability-building programs
  • Define AI champion roles across functions
  • Set up governance for ethics, data, and reuse

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do enterprises engage with AI CoE? What is the approach?

An enterprise can leverage AI CoE support through five structured stages:

EXPLORE — Deep-dive into operations, identify inefficiencies and AI-ready functions, conduct stakeholder interviews and process mapping.
STRATEGIZE — Define AI vision and priorities, run design-thinking workshops, create a roadmap for short-term wins and long-term capability.
SOURCE — Finalise requirements and KPIs, scout and validate AI startups, design proof-of-value and pilot models.
IMPLEMENT — Support PoC and pilot implementation, align with IT teams, establish performance metrics and governance.
ENABLE — Run capability-building programs, define AI champion roles, set up governance for ethics, data, and reuse.
2. What kind of ROI should I expect from AI adoption and by when?

Adopting AI across enterprise operations can improve speed, precision, and overall process quality. The return on investment varies by use case and depends on factors such as the maturity of current processes, solution capability, scale, and budget.

3. My enterprise has an annual turnover of ₹200 crores. Will AI CoE help me?

Yes. AI CoE works with both large enterprises and MSMEs across industries, helping them accelerate AI adoption regardless of size.

4. Which industries does AI CoE support?

AI CoE is industry-agnostic. Till date, CoE has enabled AI adoption across 1,000+ manufacturing and industrial organisations, including Engineering, Automotive & Auto Components, Energy & Sustainability, Logistics & Supply Chain, Agriculture, Healthcare, and more. The focus is always use-case-specific.

5. What technologies does AI CoE enable for enterprises?

AI CoE supports adoption across a broad technology stack:

  • AI & Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Automation & Robotics
  • Conversational & Language Technologies
  • IoT & IIoT
  • Sustainability & Energy AI
  • Data Engineering & Advanced Analytics
  • Cloud & Data Lake Optimisation
  • Cybersecurity & AI
  • Continuous Improvement Systems

Technology is selected only if it solves a real business problem.

6. What is the typical engagement duration?

AI CoE recommends year-long enterprise partnerships, covering use case exploration, digital roadmap creation, solution provider scouting, and implementation support. Shorter engagements are also possible for specific assessments or pilots.

7. Who supports and governs AI CoE?

AI CoE operates with strong institutional backing:

  • Department of Science & Technology, Government of Gujarat
  • Gujarat Informatics Limited
  • NASSCOM
  • Microsoft
8. What activities does AI CoE do for enterprise owners?

AI CoE does various activities that help both entities stay connected and updated with on-ground challenges and new technologies — including inviting industry leaders to relevant events and panel discussions, facilitating participation in the AI Innovation Challenge through problem statements, conducting roundtable discussions with select industry leaders on embracing technology at the management level, and more.

9. How does an industry leader get started?

To get started, we simply set up a meeting with the concerned person in the team so you can have a one-on-one discussion about the challenges you're facing and how AI can help resolve them.
