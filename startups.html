Mentorship for GrowX Accelerated Startups

Connect directly with experienced industry leaders through personalized one-on-one sessions designed to explore technical challenges and business strategies. This program offers startups an invaluable chance to navigate market dynamics, uncover emerging trends, and gain insights that drive meaningful growth.

How It Works

  • Startups share their key challenges and mentorship needs in advance to have an open, focused discussion.
  • A curated meeting is facilitated by the CoE focal point to ensure productive and meaningful dialogue.
  • Actionable insights and next steps are documented and shared with the startup to support ongoing progress.

Details of all onboarded mentors will be shared soon. Stay tuned.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The startup should be incorporated as a Private Limited Company, Partnership Firm, or Limited Liability Partnership (LLP).
  • Turnover should be less than INR 100 Crores in any of the previous financial years.
  • An entity shall be considered a startup up to 10 years from the date of incorporation.
  • The startup should be working towards innovation or improvement of existing products, services, or processes and have the potential to generate employment or create wealth.
  • An entity formed by splitting up or reconstruction of an existing business shall not be considered a startup.
