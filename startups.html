Mentorship for GrowX Accelerated Startups

Connect directly with experienced industry leaders through personalized one-on-one sessions designed to explore technical challenges and business strategies. This program offers startups an invaluable chance to navigate market dynamics, uncover emerging trends, and gain insights that drive meaningful growth.

How It Works

  • Startups share their key challenges and mentorship needs in advance to have an open, focused discussion.
  • A curated meeting is facilitated by the CoE focal point to ensure productive and meaningful dialogue.
  • Actionable insights and next steps are documented and shared with the startup to support ongoing progress.

Nikhil's contributions have been widely recognized, including multiple honors at the Innovation Congress (2020, 2021, and 2023) as one of India's most innovative leaders. He is also a TEDx speaker and the author of Courage: The Journey of an Innovator, where he shares insights on leadership, resilience, and breakthrough thinking.

Under his leadership, Maker's Lab has delivered several pioneering achievements—from discovering therapeutic molecules against COVID-19 using a combination of computational and experimental approaches, to conceptualizing India's first "meta bank." A strong advocate of inclusive technology, Nikhil has long championed the vision of enabling machines to understand and communicate in India's diverse linguistic landscape.

This vision has materialized through Project Indus, a flagship initiative to build an Indic Large Language Model (LLM). The project has positioned Tech Mahindra as a key contributor to the India AI mission—the only Global System Integrator (GSI) in the program. The open-sourced Indus model has gained significant traction and ranks among the most downloaded AI models in India.

Nikhil holds a Master's degree in Computing with a specialization in Distributed Systems from the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology (RMIT), Australia. Beyond technology, he is deeply passionate about physics and the fundamental nature of intelligence and computation.
