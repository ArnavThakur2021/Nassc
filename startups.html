Incubation Admission Benchmarks

Startups requesting infrastructure allocation and market track onboarding must strictly align with the following criteria set by administrative rules:

  • Legal Structure: Must be registered as a Private Limited Entity, standard Partnership, or a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP).
  • Ecosystem Tenure: The entity must sit under 10 years of operations computed from the certified date of incorporation.
  • Revenue Caps: Annual turnover markers must trace below INR 100 Crores within all completed fiscal years.
  • Core Objective: Operations must demonstrate core technical innovations or material enhancements over existing software, systems, or deployment mechanics.
  • Exclusion Profiles: Any venture organized by splitting up, restructuring, or renaming an existing corporate operation is strictly ineligible.
