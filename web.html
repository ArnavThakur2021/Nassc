About the Initiative

AI Centre of Excellence

A pioneering initiative by the Department of Science and Technology and Gujarat Informatics Limited, in collaboration with Nasscom , Microsoft and Google.
Aligned with the IndiaAI mission, the CoE accelerates AI adoption across SMEs, industries and citizen-centric services
Driving sector-specific AI use case creation, pilot projects, and AI model facilitation in Manufacturing, Healthcare, Agriculture, Logistics, Public Services, and more.

Public Services

AI CoE works with government departments to identify high-impact problem areas, bridge technology gaps, and facilitate the deployment of scalable AI solutions for effective governance and public service delivery.

Industries

AI CoE partners with industries to understand their operational challenges, identify the right use cases, connect them with suitable solution providers, and guide them throughout their digital transformation journey.
