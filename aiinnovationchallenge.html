Bridging Demand and Innovation

The AI Innovation Challenge serves as a unified platform empowering enterprise stakeholders, MSMEs, and government departments to address their critical operational hurdles by collaborating with growth-stage AI and deep-tech startups across India.

This model eliminates traditional experimentation cycle risks. Stakeholders submit complex real-world problems, refine them with targeted domain expert support, and co-create validated, outcome-focused solutions deployed inside native live networks.

Startup Eligibility Criteria

To align with the challenge ecosystem guidelines, applicant entities must satisfy the following:

  • Incorporation Profile: Registered as a Private Limited Company, LLP, or Partnership Firm within the last 10 years.
  • Revenue Ceiling: Annual turnover must not exceed ₹25 Crores (or up to ₹100 Crores depending on specific sector challenge tiers) in any prior financial year.
  • Core Purpose: Working directly toward innovation, scaling, or measurable transformation of existing products, services, or workflow pipelines.
  • Operational Rule: The entity must not be formed by splitting up or reconstructing an existing pre-established business framework.

Ecosystem Benefits

For Government Departments: Enhances service delivery models, maximizes fund utilization efficiency, and accelerates objective data-driven public choices.

For MSMEs & Enterprises: Offers immediate access to targeted innovations without requiring high capital infrastructure risks or extensive internal R&D loops.

For Deep-Tech Startups: Provides direct customer market access channels, production deployment reference validation, and clear technology validation opportunities.
