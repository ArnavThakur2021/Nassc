About the Center of Excellence

Driving institutional adoption of Deep-Tech solutions by bridging innovative startup architectures with corporate pipelines and public governance models.

Who We Are

NASSCOM (National Association of Software and Service Companies) is the premier trade association and chamber of commerce for the tech industry in India. As a non-profit organization, NASSCOM spearheads the development of the digital economy, guiding the country towards achieving its global potential as a supreme technical innovation hub.

The NASSCOM Center of Excellence - IoT & AI is a strategic flagship initiative sustained in joint alignment with national and state government authorities. Based in GIFT City, Gandhinagar, the CoE acts as an accelerated collaborative sanctuary created to cultivate deep-tech intelligence systems, incubate startup talent, and address production vulnerabilities across public systems and enterprise value chains.

Our Core Vision

We envision a seamlessly integrated digital landscape where complex AI engines and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) protocols step out of isolated labs and manifest onto the live operational floors of micro, small, medium, and large-scale industries.

By curating structured integration spaces, providing shared cloud compute infrastructures, and standardizing security reference metrics, we empower ecosystem stakeholders to adapt to disruptive technological paradigms securely, cost-effectively, and at unprecedented scale.

Strategic Objectives

Accelerating AI Adoption

Driving deep artificial intelligence workflows into the operational core of public delivery services, ensuring rapid data handling and data-driven administrative systems.

Industry-Startup Cohesion

Designing targeted procurement channels that allow legacy industries to match operational issues directly with validated deep-tech startup cohorts.

Infrastructure Enablement

Lowering capital barriers for growth-stage ventures by supplying access to specialized prototyping environments, high-performance computing, and validation tools.

Our Operational Pillars

How the Center of Excellence impacts tech transformation ecosystems.

Policy & Standards Alignment

We work tightly alongside public policy developers to explain emerging guidelines such as the DPDP Act and state-level IT/ITeS incentives, directly helping companies remain structurally aligned.

Ecosystem Capability Building

Hosting regular technical design thinking forums, artificial intelligence roundtables, and specialized innovation challenges to consistently upgrade the digital acumen of engineering talent networks.

Global Market Routing

Providing mature deep-tech solutions with an expansive global platform by leveraging NASSCOM's extensive international enterprise relations, expanding cross-border commercial opportunities.

Empowering digital transformation through collaborative deep-tech implementation models.
