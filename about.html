Who We Are

The AI Centre of Excellence is a pioneering initiative by the Department of Science and Technology, Gujarat Informatics Limited, in collaboration with Nasscom and in support of Microsoft and Google.

Aligned with IndiaAI mission, the CoE accelerates AI adoption across SMEs, industries and citizen-centric services. It drives sector-specific AI use-case creations, pilot projects, and AI model facilitation in key areas such as Manufacturing, Healthcare, Agriculture, Logistics, Public services etc.


Key Functions
Technology adoption in SMEs, Public and Private Sectors
Solution Co-creation
Deep Tech Startup Acceleration
AI Experience Zone of Cutting-Edge Solutions
AI Literacy and Training Workshops
Infrastructure Support

What We Do

We source problem statements directly from departments and MSMEs, ensuring challenges come straight from actual end users.

We use authentic, user-provided datasets, making solutions accurate, and deployment-ready.

We open these challenges on a national Innovation Platform, enabling competitive participation from startups across the country to bring the best ideas forward.

Shortlisted startups are then handheld through the journey, receiving support and funding from Government/MSMEs to build PoCs, run pilots, and move toward full-scale implementation.
